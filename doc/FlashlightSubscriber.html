Class: FlashlightSubscriber

FlashlightSubscriber(ros, topicName)

FlashlightSubscriber subscribes to a ROS topic and turns the flashlight on on command. Used source: https://codepen.io/adamrifai/pen/YLxjKa The data should be from a topic with message type ROS std_msgs/Bool message.
